Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects the information you provide directly when you create an account and log expenses. This includes your name, email address, and the expense data you enter — such as amount, category, date, and description.

We do not collect payment card information, bank account details, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power Spendly's expense tracking features — including generating summaries, category breakdowns, and filtering by date range.

We do not use your personal or expense data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored on secure servers managed by Spendly. We employ standard encryption and access controls to protect your information from unauthorized access.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@spendly.app.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use trusted third-party services for infrastructure, analytics, and email delivery. These providers are contractually bound to handle your data only for the purposes of delivering their services to us.

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise any of your data rights, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
